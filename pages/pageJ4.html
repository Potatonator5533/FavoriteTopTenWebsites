My Top Ten Favorite Board Games: #4 & 3

#4: Chess
Chess is a pure strategy game (which I enjoy) that fully resolves around the decisions made throughout the game. Chess is also a popular game in which many people know how to play making it a very good game to play at many occasions.
#3: Risk
Risk is a very fun game that can be played for hours on a single game (which I also enjoy). The strategy and luck involved in this game I believe is quite balanced but can quickly turn on you if you aren't careful, hence the name.
