My Top Ten Favorite Board Games: #10 & 9

#10: Dice City
Dice city is a game that I sometimes play when I don't want to think too much. As the game title kind of gives away the aspect of the game, Dice city contains a lot of dice rolling and choices throughout the game to determine how you want to build your city.
#9: Planet Planet is a fun game to play whenever I don't want to be thinking too hard (similar to Planet). I quite like the card designs, the aspect and goal of the game but due to the size of the box and the pieces, I wouldn't recommend taking it outside of your residence.
