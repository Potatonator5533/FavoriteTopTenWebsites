My Top Ten Favorite Board Games: #2 & 1

#2: Scythe
I have only had Scythe for a few months now but it is already one of my favorite games because of how much thought was put into this complex game. I especially enjoy the aesthetic looks of the game pieces and the board. I don't care if it takes 10-20 minutes to set up, it's fun.
#1: Catan
Catan is my overall favorite game mainly because I have had and played it for several years and have gotten four expansions for the game. What I also really enjoy about this game is how much your strategy will change depending on the rolls of the dice.
