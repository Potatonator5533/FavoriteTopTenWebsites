MMy Top Ten Favorite Splatoon 3 Weapons: #4 & 3

#4: Z+F Splat Charger
The original Splat Charger is great, but all it needs is a bit more protection. That's where the Zekko + Firefin varient comes in. With splash walls to keep you safe and the Triple Inkstrike to take out foes out of your range, you'll be a force to be reckoned with. Of course, it all comes with the Splat Charger's all-rounder range and kill power.
#3: Tentatek Splattershot
It's almost impossible to go through matches and matches without noticing at least one Tentatek Splattershot, but that's how good of an all-rounder weapon it is. Combining the standard classic that is Splattershot and Splat Bombs, it's just as reliable as PB with J. All it needs is the bread, and that's where the Triple inkstrike Special comes in, which can no doubt decimate enemy defenses in no time flat.
