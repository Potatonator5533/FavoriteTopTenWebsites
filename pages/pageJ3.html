My Top Ten Favorite Board Games: #6 & 5

#6: Monopoly
One of the main reasons Monoploy is on my list is mainly because of nostalgia. Don't get me wrong, this game is an expert in getting families together and then immediately separating them but it's not higher in the list because it is mostly based off of luck.
#5: Quoridor
Quoridor is similar to chess but with even easier rules. Despite simple rules, Quoridor is still a very challenging game. I also really enjoy that the game is small and compact which allows me to take it to many parties without much problem.
