My Top Ten Favorite Board Games: #8 & 7

#8: Ticket to Ride
I have only played Ticket to Ride a few times and out of those few times, I enjoyed the game. I also enjoy the aesthetics of just about everything in this game but the reason why it is placed low in this list is the simple reason of not playing enough and learning everything there is know about it.
#7: Pandemic
Pandemic is quite a fun game because it is quite uncommon for all the players to be siding together to fight the game itself. The design of the game board is also quite good but is placed quite low in this list is similar to Ticket to Ride, I haven't played enough.
