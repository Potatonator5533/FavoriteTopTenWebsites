My Top Ten Favorite Splatoon 3 Weapons: #8 & 7

#8: Hydra Splatling
Splatlings are known for their concentrated power, and the Hydra Splatling is no exception. Don't be fooled by its wind up time. With the right spacing and a clear view, its devastating on the field to your oppponents. Auto bombs can keep opponents distracted as you charge, and once you got enough power for a Booyah Bomb, you can kiss the bad guys goodbye!
#7: Painbrush
One of the brand new weapons that were introduced in Splatoon 3, this one has the longest range of the 3 brush-class weapons. It does take a bit of chargup to start swinging, but it deals damage fast. Curling bombs help you get to the action fast, and the Wave Breaker will make it just hard enough for opponents to evade your swings.
